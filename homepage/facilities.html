Our Campus Facilities

Home
Priority Parking

Reserved parking spaces near key buildings ensure convenience and proximity for individuals requiring enhanced accessibility. Strategically placed, these spaces help create a hassle-free campus experience.

Library

Our library houses a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources. With dedicated study zones and research-friendly tools, it's the ideal environment for intellectual growth and discovery.

Laboratory

State-of-the-art laboratories empower students to turn theoretical concepts into practical solutions. Well-equipped for a variety of disciplines, they nurture innovation and experimental learning.

Outdoor Amphitheater

An inspiring open-air auditorium where ideas and talents take center stage. With tiered seating that blends functionality with comfort, this space is perfect for cultural events, discussions, and lively student gatherings under the sky.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria offers a diverse menu of freshly prepared meals and snacks. With a lively ambiance and hygienic standards, it's the go-to spot for students to unwind and recharge. Healthy options and hearty treats cater to everyone's taste.

NCC/NSS

The NCC/NSS units offer students a chance to engage in national service and leadership programs. Through community projects, camps, and drills, they instill discipline, social responsibility, and a sense of patriotism.

Inclusive Resources

Wheelchair ramps, aquaguard, elevators ensure seamless mobility, making every corner of the campus accessible to all students and visitors. Designed with precision, they reflect our commitment to fostering inclusivity and independence for everyone.

Smart Room

Step into the future of learning with smart classrooms equipped with interactive boards, high-definition projectors, and advanced audio-visual tools. These technology-enabled spaces make education immersive, collaborative, and impactful.

Rest Room

Our accessible restrooms are thoughtfully designed for convenience, ensuring a dignified and comfortable experience for everyone. They are maintained to the highest standards of cleanliness and usability for all.
